# Republic Explorer User Guide

Welcome to the **Republic Explorer**! This guide will help you navigate the directory, discover projects, and contribute to the Republic ecosystem.

## 🚀 Overview

Republic Explorer is the definitive directory for projects, protocols, and teams building on the RepublicAI network. Whether you're a user looking for new tools or a builder wanting to showcase your work, this platform is for you.

---

## 🔍 How to Browse and Search

### Discover Projects

The home page features a grid of all approved projects. Each card gives you a quick overview of the project's purpose and status.

- **Search**: Use the search bar at the top to find projects by name, category, or tags (e.g., "AI", "Infrastructure", "DeFi").
- **Filter**: Click on category pills (like "Infrastructure", "Ecosystem", etc.) to narrow down the list.

### Project Details

Click on any project card to open a detailed view. Here you'll find:

- A full description of the project.
- Links to their Website, Twitter, Discord, and GitHub.
- Current status (e.g., "Live", "In Development").
- Key metrics or updates.

---

## 💳 Connecting Your Wallet

To submit or update your own projects, you'll need to connect your wallet.

1. Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top navigation bar.
2. Select your preferred wallet (e.g., Leap, Keplr) and approve the connection for the **Republic AI Testnet**.
3. Once connected, your wallet address will appear in the navigation bar.

---

## 📤 Submitting a New Project

Have you built something on Republic? Add it to the explorer!

1. **Connect your wallet** (see above).
2. Click **Submit Project** in the navigation bar or the hero section.
3. Fill out the submission form with:
   - **Project Name & Description**: Be clear and concise.
   - **Category & Tags**: Helping others find your project.
   - **Links**: Website and social media profiles.
   - **Visuals**: Logo and a feature image.
4. Click **Submit for Review**.

> [!NOTE]
> Your project will enter a **"Pending"** state. It won't be visible to the public until an administrator approves it.

---

## 🔄 Updating Your Project

If you need to change your project's info or status:

1. **Connect the same wallet** used for submission.
2. Go to the **Update Project** tab.
3. Select your project from the list.
4. Make your changes and save.

---

## 🛠️ Ecosystem Features

Beyond the explorer, you can also access these tools from the sidebar:

- **GPU Miner**: Access resources for GPU mining on Republic.
- **Validator**: Learn how to become a validator and support network security.

---

## 🔔 Notifications

Keep an eye on the bottom right of your screen. You'll receive real-time notifications when:

- Your project is approved and goes live.
- Your submission requires further updates.
- Your wallet is connected or disconnected.
